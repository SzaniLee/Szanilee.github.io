    About Me.

    Hi there!

    I am Alexandra (Szani) Lee, a passionate User Experience & User Interface designer / Sr. Product Designer practicing The Design Thinking process to solve complex problems. Currently I live in Colorado, USA but I was born and raised in Hungary. I decided to step on this path as I truly enjoy the investigative aspects of research and design and my never-quenching thirst for being able to create not just aesthetic, but problem solving products. I love being involved in the entire design process - from defining problems, all the way to testing prototypes with proposed solutions to them. I am passionate about not just expanding my own skillset and continously learning about new design trends and techniques, I find it fulfilling when I am able to help aspiring UX Designers while they are transitioning to the tech field from a different one.

    Fun fact about me: My husband and I are a multicultural & international couple (Korean-American + Hungarian American).
