    About me.

    Greetings, Earthlings!

    I am astronaut Szani Lee, a bear-tastic Sr. Product Designer. Living in space requires innovative and efficient solutions to problems that arise. My design philosophy revolves around this idea. I firmly believe that understanding and addressing user problems are the foundation of successful products. Just as a well-designed space suit ensures comfort and mobility in zero gravity, I aim to develop products that seamlessly adapt to users' requirements while providing a delightful experience. Attention to detail is paramount in product design, much like every component of a spacecraft meticulously designed to withstand the unforgiving conditions of space. I bring that same level of precision to every project, ensuring that no aspect is overlooked. Let's join forces and create something truly extraordinary that surpasses expectations.
