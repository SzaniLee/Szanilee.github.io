    About Me.

    Hi there!

    I am Alexandra (Szani) Lee, a passionate User Experience & User Interface designer / Product Designer practicing The Design Thinking process to solve complex problems. Currently I live in Arizona but I was born and raised in Hungary. I decided to step on this path as I truly enjoy the investigative aspects of research and design and my never-quenching thirst for being able to create not just aesthetic, but problem solving products. I love being involved in the entire design process - from defining problems, all the way to testing prototypes with proposed solutions to them. I am passionate about not just expanding my own skillset and continously learning about new design trends and techniques, I find it fulfilling when I am able to help aspiring UX Designers while they are transitioning to the tech field from a different one.

    Fun fact about me: I met my husband online "accidentally", we fought through a long distance relationship (USA - Europe) for 2 years then we finally got married once we graduated from our universities. We are a multicultural & international couple (Korean-American + Hungarian American) with lots of love and respect for each other! :) <3

    Moral of the story: I believe that happiness is something we create with passion in our hearts & it's up to us how we paint our life.
