    About Me.

    Greetings, Earthlings!

    I am astronaut Alexandra (Szani) Lee, a bear-tastic Sr. Product Designer. Living and working in space requires innovative and efficient solutions to problems that arise. My design philosophy revolves around this idea. I believe understanding and solving user problems are key to a successful product. With my problem-solving skills, I can create out-of-this-world products with confidence that are intuitive, easy to use, and tailored to the needs of their users - just like the design of a space suit, which should be comfortable and provide a full range of motion while in zero gravity. Attention to detail is crucial in product design, just as every component of a spacecraft must be specifically designed to withstand the harsh conditions of space. If you're looking for a designer who can bring that same level of attention to detail and understanding of user needs to your project, let's work together and create something truly out of this world.

    Fun fact about me: My husband and I are a multicultural & international couple (Korean-American + Hungarian American).
